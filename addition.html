Addition

Addition is the combining of two or more amounts, objects or item, where your results is dependent on these two or more variables to produce a defined total.

+

'+' represents the process of addition, and the symbol is known as plus. An example being 4 + 3 , read as "4 plus 3"

Addition sentence

A mathematical expression that shows two or more values, named in the math wrold, addends, added together makes the sum, the total. This is an addition sentence. With '=' being the equal sign, serving as a representation of the left side is equivalent to the right.
